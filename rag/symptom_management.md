# Symptom Management

## Common Symptoms and Solutions

### Headache
**Causes:** Electrolyte imbalance, dehydration, caffeine withdrawal
**Solutions:**
- Increase salt intake
- Drink more water
- Bone broth
- Rest

### Dizziness
**Causes:** Low sodium, low blood pressure
**Solutions:**
- Salt liberally
- Stand up slowly
- Stay hydrated
- Eat adequate fat

### Fatigue
**Causes:** Adaptation phase, insufficient calories, electrolytes
**Solutions:**
- Eat more fat
- Check calorie intake
- Electrolytes
- Be patient (adaptation takes time)

### Muscle Cramps
**Causes:** Low magnesium, potassium, sodium
**Solutions:**
- More salt
- Bone broth
- Eat more meat
- Magnesium supplement if needed

### Constipation
**Causes:** Fiber withdrawal, dehydration, adaptation
**Solutions:**
- More water
- More fat
- Bone broth
- Movement/walking
- Usually resolves in 2-3 weeks

### Diarrhea
**Causes:** Too much fat too fast, adaptation
**Solutions:**
- Reduce fat temporarily
- Add more lean protein
- Give body time to adjust
- Gradual fat increase

### Brain Fog
**Causes:** Low sodium, glucose withdrawal, adaptation
**Solutions:**
- More salt
- Be patient
- Rest adequately
- Usually clears week 2-3

### Low Energy
**Causes:** Not fat-adapted yet, insufficient calories
**Solutions:**
- Eat more
- More fat especially
- Give it time
- Check sleep quality

### Nausea
**Causes:** Too much fat, gallbladder adjusting
**Solutions:**
- Reduce fat temporarily
- Smaller meals more often
- Lean meats initially

## When to Seek Help
- Symptoms persisting beyond 4 weeks
- Severe symptoms
- Heart palpitations that don't resolve
- Significant weakness
- Unable to eat
