# Carnivore Basics

## What is Carnivore Diet?
A zero-carb, animal-based diet consisting only of meat, fish, eggs, and animal fats.

## Core Principles
- Eat only animal products
- No plant foods
- No processed foods
- No seed oils
- No added sugars

## Allowed Foods (Strict)
- Beef (all cuts)
- Pork
- Lamb
- Fish and seafood
- Eggs
- Animal fats (tallow, lard)
- Salt
- Water

## Allowed Foods (Relaxed)
All strict foods plus:
- Butter and ghee
- Hard cheeses
- Heavy cream
- Black coffee

## Benefits Reported
- Weight loss
- Reduced inflammation
- Mental clarity
- Stable energy
- Improved digestion

## Adaptation Period
First 2-4 weeks may include:
- Electrolyte imbalance
- Fatigue
- Digestive changes
- Cravings

These typically resolve as body adapts to ketosis.
