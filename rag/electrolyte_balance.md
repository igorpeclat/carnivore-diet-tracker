# Electrolyte Balance

## Why Electrolytes Matter on Carnivore
Low carb diets reduce insulin, causing kidneys to excrete more sodium.
This triggers loss of other electrolytes (potassium, magnesium).

## Key Electrolytes

### Sodium
- **Need:** 4-7g daily (more than standard diet)
- **Sources:** Salt on food, bone broth
- **Deficiency signs:** Headache, fatigue, dizziness, cramps

### Potassium
- **Need:** 3-4g daily
- **Sources:** Meat (especially beef), fish
- **Deficiency signs:** Muscle weakness, cramps, heart palpitations

### Magnesium
- **Need:** 300-400mg daily
- **Sources:** Fish, dark meat poultry
- **Deficiency signs:** Cramps, twitching, poor sleep

## Symptoms of Imbalance
- Dizziness when standing
- Muscle cramps
- Headaches
- Fatigue
- Heart palpitations
- Brain fog

## Solutions
1. Salt food liberally
2. Drink bone broth daily
3. Eat organ meats weekly
4. Consider supplementation if symptoms persist

## Warning Signs (Seek Help)
- Persistent heart palpitations
- Severe muscle weakness
- Fainting
