# Carnivore Recipes

## Ribeye with Butter
**Level:** Strict (Relaxed with butter)
**Ingredients:**
- 300g ribeye steak
- 30g butter
- Salt to taste

**Steps:**
1. Bring steak to room temperature
2. Season generously with salt
3. Sear in hot pan 3-4 min each side
4. Rest 5 minutes
5. Top with butter

**Macros:** ~650 kcal, 50g protein, 50g fat

## Bacon and Eggs
**Level:** Strict
**Ingredients:**
- 4 strips bacon
- 3 eggs
- Salt

**Steps:**
1. Cook bacon until crispy
2. Fry eggs in bacon fat
3. Season with salt

**Macros:** ~500 kcal, 30g protein, 40g fat

---

## Ground Beef Patties
**Level:** Strict
**Ingredients:**
- 400g ground beef (80/20)
- Salt

**Steps:**
1. Form into patties
2. Season with salt
3. Cook 4-5 min each side
4. Rest before serving

**Macros:** ~800 kcal, 80g protein, 55g fat
